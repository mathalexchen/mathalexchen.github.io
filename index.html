About Me

Welcome to my page! I am a graduate student in applied mathematics at UCLA, graduating in June 2011. My current research projects are image segmentation, atomic force microscopy, and landscape modeling. My advisor is Andrea Bertozzi. I was also a visiting student at ENS de Cachan, France under the direction of Jean-Michel Morel. For more information about me or my research projects, please see my CV and "Research Interests."
